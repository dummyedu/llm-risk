# Risk Application Notes

This page records possible implications for the local `risk` project.

## Boundary

`risk` is a later application target. This wiki does not modify the `risk`
repository. Notes here are research outputs, not implementation instructions.

## Current Hypotheses

- Application hypothesis: The `risk` project's ERC/catalog/tool gateway may be a
  lightweight semantic layer for controlled LLM business exploration.
- Application hypothesis: Palantir-style ontology and AIP eval ideas may help
  frame how business capabilities are exposed to an assistant.
- Application hypothesis: FDE-style delivery methods may help structure how new
  business slices are researched, modeled, validated, and shipped.

# Questions

## Durable Research Questions

- What exactly does Palantir mean by ontology in operational systems?
- Where does ontology-augmented generation differ materially from RAG?
- Which parts of AIP are model capability, which parts are governance, and which
  parts are delivery method?
- How do FDE, bootcamp, and customer deployment practices change software
  productization?
- What does OSDK imply for enterprise application development?
- Which Palantir ideas are general enterprise-intelligence methods, and which
  are specific to Palantir's product and sales model?

## Application Questions For Risk

- Application hypothesis: Could the `risk` project's ERC/catalog/tool gateway
  become a lightweight ontology-like semantic layer?
- Application hypothesis: Which Palantir-style eval or guardrail ideas can be
  tested against the `risk` assistant without adopting Palantir's full model?
- Application hypothesis: Which delivery practices from FDE or bootcamp are
  useful for onboarding new business slices into the `risk` assistant?

# Research Principles

## Research Stays Research

The wiki exists to understand enterprise-intelligence methods before turning
them into project decisions.

## Slow Is Accurate

Prefer one source file, or one small coherent bundle, per session. The goal is
to learn and discuss, not to maximize ingestion volume.

## Claims Need Labels

Every durable claim should be labeled as one of:

- Source fact
- Author opinion
- User judgment
- LLM inference
- Application hypothesis

## Discussion Is Part Of The Artifact

When the user corrects, prioritizes, or challenges a source, record that as a
discussion note in the review package.

## Applications Are Hypotheses

Connections to the `risk` project are useful, but they remain hypotheses until
validated in that project.

## Prefer Durable Pages

Create formal wiki pages only for recurring concepts, methods, cases, source
audits, application notes, or durable questions.
